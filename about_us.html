A brief History

Founded in 1976, Jahazing was one of the most elite, luxurious, transcontinental airlines of all time. A group of 4 individuals carved their rough idea into a reality by launching their first flight from Lahore(Pakistan) to Munich (Germany) on 6th November 1976.
Many organisations have signed us as their travel partner. The Cricket World Cup winning team of Australia in 1987 travelled back to Australia from Kolkata(India) with our airlines. The famous Indian Tycoon Dhirubhai Ambani, founder of Reliance and the father of one of the Asia's richest men Mukesh Ambani, have travelled to Europe numerously with our airline.
Having their Head Office in Karachi(Pakistan), Jahazing is operating 110 global offices to serve and facilitate their passengers, and to ensure the safe, comfortable journey of their valuable customers.
